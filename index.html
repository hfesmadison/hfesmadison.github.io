Designing systems

I'm a final-year Industrial Engineering student at UW–Madison with a focus in Human Factors. My work sits at the intersection of engineering rigor and human-centered thinking — studying how people interact with tools, environments, and systems, then making those interactions safer and more efficient.

My core interest is in ergonomics and workplace safety: reducing injury risk, improving workflow design, and applying biomechanical and cognitive principles to real environments.
